Privacy Policy for Fingerspell Go

Last updated: July 26, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Fingerspell Go is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best ASL learning experience:

Anonymous Mode (Default)

  • Practice Data: Your learning progress, scores, and achievements are stored locally on your device only
  • App Preferences: Settings like difficulty level, speed preferences, and enabled word categories
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers

Sign in with Apple (Optional)

  • Apple ID: When you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive only a unique, anonymized identifier
  • Email (Optional): Only if you choose to share it through Apple's privacy controls
  • Name (Optional): Only if you choose to share it through Apple's privacy controls

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: Track your learning progress and customize your ASL practice experience
  • Improve the App: Understand how features are used to make improvements (no personal data)
  • Sync Data: If you sign in with Apple, sync your progress across your devices

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All practice data is stored securely on your device using Apple's Core Data framework
  • iCloud Sync: If you sign in with Apple, encrypted data may sync through your personal iCloud account
  • No Third-Party Servers: We do not store your personal data on external servers

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your learning data stays with you.

5. Children's Privacy

Fingerspell Go is safe for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app operates in anonymous mode by default and includes age verification during onboarding.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is accessible through the app's settings and progress screens
  • Delete: You can reset your progress or delete the app to remove all data
  • Control: Choose anonymous mode or sign in with Apple - it's your choice

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "last updated" date.
